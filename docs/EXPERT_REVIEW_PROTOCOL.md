# Expert Review Protocol

This step requires a real independent domain expert and must not be simulated or replaced with generated testimony.

## Reviewer qualifications

Recruit at least one practitioner or researcher in power systems, water utilities, emergency medicine, telecom resilience, transport operations, or critical-infrastructure risk. Record role, years of relevant experience, and conflicts of interest with consent.

## Blinded evaluation

Present ten minimized cascades in randomized order: five from evolutionary search and five equal-budget baseline cases. Hide the generating method. Ask the reviewer to score each from 1–5 on:

- Physical/logical plausibility
- Novelty relative to standard exercises
- Causal trace coherence
- Operational actionability
- Harm from false confidence

## Required record

- Exact model version and seed
- Reviewer instructions
- Raw scores and comments, including criticism
- Which scenarios the reviewer rejected and why
- Whether assumptions were changed after review

## Success criterion

Evolutionary scenarios should outperform baselines on novelty without materially underperforming on plausibility. Report all results, not only favorable comments. One review is prototype evidence, not scientific consensus.
