# Limitations

Black Swan Forge is a synthetic research prototype and does not predict real cities. All results are plausible under the assumptions of this model.

- Topology and parameters are demonstrative and have not been calibrated against utility, hospital, telecom, transport, or emergency-service data.
- Scalar capacity omits voltage/frequency, hydraulic pressure/quality, traffic flow, inventories, queueing, and clinical acuity.
- Dependency effects are deterministic thresholds; real failures are uncertain, state-dependent, spatial, and correlated.
- Recovery is simplified and does not model crews, parts, operator decisions, public behavior, or adversaries.
- Population and economic metrics are normalized illustrative mappings, not estimates.
- Intervention costs are relative research units, not currency or implementation estimates.
- The optimizer searches single interventions. Search fitness and novelty signatures are prototype formulations.
- Causal parentage is valid within the executable model; it is not proof of causality in a real city.

Real deployment requires validated infrastructure data, domain experts, security review, uncertainty quantification, and governance appropriate to sensitive critical-infrastructure information.
